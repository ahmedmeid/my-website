About me

Hi, my name is Ahmed (Arabic: أحمد pronunciation: [ˈʔæħmæd])
I am from Cairo, Egypt and I am 38 years old.
I am married and I have two kids, Habiba 6 years, and Mohamed 2 years.
I am a Senior Solutions Architect at NEOM since October 2021, and I have over 14 years of experience in Information Systems Architecture, Software Design, and Java Development.
I am an expert in IT Solutions Design, Systems Development and Enterprise Applications Integration, I have worked in several large-scale IT projects in multinational companies like: Ericsson, Atos, and Accenture in different countries in North Africa, in Libya and Morocco, and in the middle east, in Dubai, Kuwait, Iraq, and KSA.
Recently, I have worked in several interseting projects for building the world's first cognitive city using AI, Data Science, and Machine Learning technonlogies.
I have a bachelor degree in Pure Mathematics and Computer Science from Menoufia University, and a postgraduate diploma in Software Engineering from Information Technology Institute in Cairo.
